Charisma Life Tabernacle's

Vision and mission

Welcome to Charisma Life Tabernacle

Charisma Life Tabernacle is one church with one assignment in three different locations (Duduza, Pretoria and Johannesburg South) in the Gauteng Province. The church is under the anointed leadership of Kabelo and Nompumelelo Moroke. Charisma Life Tabernacle is a Christ-centred, Word based and vibrant community of believers that is committed to equipping the saints given to our charge to fulfil their God-given callings, giftings, and ministries; hence the name Charisma which means Grace-Gift.



We are a doctrinal and dynamic household of faith with a passion to:

MATURE saints IN CHRIST

MATURE leaders IN CHRIST

MATURE churches and ministries IN CHRIST



Our VISION as Charisma Life Tabernacle is to:

RECOGNISE WHO YOU ARE IN CHRIST

RAISE WHO YOU ARE IN CHRIST

SEND WHO YOU ARE IN CHRIST



Our MISSION as Charisma Life Tabernacle:

TEACHINGS OF CHRIST

EQUIPPING IN CHRIST

ACTIVATE IN CHRIST

MATURE IN CHRIST

SEND THROUGH CHRIST
